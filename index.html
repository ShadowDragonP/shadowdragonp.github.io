About

I do all kinds of things in my free time, and I really doing what I do. Over the years, I have struggled through hardships, clinged into determination, and fought software trial time limits in order to perfect what I originally did as hobbies into hard-earned skills.

Long story short, I began programming in 2015, when I was in 8th grade. In a state competition I participated, my event was to design a video game. So I gathered three of my friends and formed a team called "WTF Interprises." Our game didn't place top three, but at that point I knew that programming games was going to be a big part of my life. The summer after 8th grade, a began making my own games, this time under a new company name called "YummyFruits Studios." Also, during 8th grade, I became interested in digital music production. My first album was released June 2, 2016, and making music became my second hobby. During the music production process, I also had to learn how to use various image editing software so that I could design the album artwork. With programming, music production, and digital imaging being my main three powers, I realized that YummyFruits Studios wasn't large enough to cover all of that. After all, it was original made for game developing. In late 2016, I founded ShadowDragon Studios, a company that contained all works I made in all three sectors. In 2017, the ShadowDragon Studios official website was launched, and it is currently expanding to cover video editing, 3D modeling, animations, and software development.
