Singles - 2017

The year of singles

About This Year

Ever since high school began, I haven't had much time to make songs. In 2016, I still had a little bit of free time, but when 2017 came by, I had a bunch of exams to study for and as well as a ton of homework. I thought that I wouldn't have time to make music, but fortunately, 10th Grade was much easier than 9th grade, leaving me a lot of time to produce music in late 2017.

Chicken Anthem

I met a person a while ago who invented a language called Chickenese.
He taught me the ways of a chicken, and I made him this song for
gratitude. It was made only two weeks after Steel Force was made,
so they may sound similar.

Uplifting Dreams

"Live while the good times last."
This was an experiment with happy hardcore. I kinda lost my train of
thought halfway through the song, so it gets kinda random. I ended
up having to cut the song short, but I guess it turned out okay.
Unlike my other songs, this one is at 175 BPM and is in a major key.

Fantasy

"Fly away, can I fly to another world..."
I really like this one. It sounds powerful and emotional. I don't know
why, but whenever I hear the part starting at 00:51, I get really weird
vibes. Like I'm forgetting something important.

Innocentia

"Maybe if I close my eyes, this nightmare will be over."
'Innocentia' is Latin for 'innocence.'I rarely ever make calm songs,
since they're not my style, but I'm glad this one turned out right.
Let's just say that I wanted something more fierce from 2:35 and onward.

Equilibrium War

"Someday, you will have to fight to maintain balance; a war in which
no one loses and no one wins."
This one was more like my style. I particularly like this one because
of I've always dreamed of making music like this one.

Equilibrium War (Bass Boosted)

"Literally lit."
One of my friends showed me how to make bass boosted versions of
songs, so I made one using Equilibrium War. This sounds awesome.
