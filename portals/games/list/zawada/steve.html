A "palindrome" is a word or number that is read the same way forwards or backwards.

On a digital clock, "1:11" would be a palindrome, since "111" is also "111" backwards. Other examples would include "2:22", "3:33", "4:44", "5:55"... you get the idea.

At what time would the clock display the next palindrome? Write your answer in letters.

(Ex. 1:11 is "oneoneone," 2:22 is "twotwotwo")

If you think the answer is "666", it proves that you're a Satanist.
